# Connect

There are many ways to participate in our work.

<div class="editorial-grid">

<div class="editorial-item">

**[Attend](/#projects)** Join our seminars, unconferences, and local events. Open to researchers, practitioners, and anyone interested in AI safety.

</div>

<div class="editorial-item">

**[Volunteer](mailto:team@horizonomega.org?subject=Volunteering)** Help with seminar operations, research briefs, speaker outreach, and video editing. Training and templates provided.

</div>

<div class="editorial-item">

**[Partner](mailto:team@horizonomega.org?subject=Partnership)** Collaborate with us as a university, lab, NGO, or standards body. We co‑host sessions, share speakers, and build pilots together.

</div>

<div class="editorial-item">

**[Support](/donate)** Donations, sponsorships, and in‑kind contributions (venue hosting, captioning, editing, design) help us expand our work.

</div>

<div class="editorial-item">

**[Follow](https://horizonomega.substack.com/)** Stay updated via our newsletter. Also on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/horizonomega.org).

</div>

<div class="editorial-item">

**[Propose](mailto:team@horizonomega.org?subject=Project%20idea)** Have a project idea or collaboration in mind? We're open to new initiatives that advance AI safety.

</div>

</div>
